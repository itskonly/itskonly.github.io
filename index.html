01 // About

The Journey So Far

I'm Kaustubh Swami — a Senior System Engineer with 3.5 years of experience in automation (YAML-based frameworks) at an MNC, building a parallel life in offensive security. I wake up at 4:30 AM not because I have to, but because becoming the world's best at anything demands that kind of commitment.

My path into cybersecurity is driven by a simple belief: understanding how systems break is the only way to truly protect them. Web Application Penetration Testing pulled me in hardest — it's where logic, creativity, and persistence collide.

Every HTB machine I solve, every CTF challenge I document, every automation script I build — they're not hobbies. They're deliberate reps toward a goal I take completely seriously.

Beyond security, I'm learning Python and Java, exploring AI-powered business ideas, and building systems that compound over time. This site is the honest documentation of that grind — mistakes, lessons, and all.
